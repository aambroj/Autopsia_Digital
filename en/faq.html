Frequently asked questions

Common questions before requesting a Product Autopsy.

What exactly is a Product Autopsy?

It is a strategic diagnosis of a digital product that is not selling, converting, getting users or giving you enough confidence to keep investing.

What types of products do you analyze?

Apps, websites, landing pages, SaaS products, online courses, ebooks, newsletters, communities, internal tools, prototypes and digital ideas.

Does the product need to be launched?

No. We can also analyze an idea, prototype, document, early landing page or commercial presentation.

Do you guarantee sales?

No. The service does not guarantee sales, users, investment, traffic or financial results. It provides strategic analysis, clarity and practical recommendations.

Is the analysis done by AI?

AI may be used as support for research, structure or analysis acceleration, but the final judgement and strategic review are human.

What will I receive?

You will receive a report with diagnosis, detected problems, opportunities, recommended actions and final verdict: relaunch, reposition, pause or shut down.

How long does it take?

It depends on the plan and complexity. Initial reference: Express 48-72 hours, Full 3-5 business days and Premium 5-10 business days.

What does the market study include?

It includes strategic external research based on public information: competitors, alternatives, visible prices, commercial messaging and opportunities.

What does the market study not include?

It does not include paid surveys, large-scale interviews, private database purchases or advanced statistical research.

Will my idea remain confidential?

Yes. Project information is treated with discretion and will not be published as a case study without explicit permission.

Can I request changes to the report?

You can request a reasonable clarification if something is not clear. Unlimited revisions or full redesigns are not included unless agreed separately.

How do I request the service?

You can start from the order page or email aambroj@yahoo.es.

Contact

To request a Product Autopsy, write directly to the official email address.

Official email

aambroj@yahoo.es

This is the main contact channel. If someone requests a diagnosis from the website, the button will open an email addressed to this address.

Send request

What to include

  • Product name.
  • URL, demo or description.
  • Product type: app, website, SaaS, course, ebook, tool or idea.
  • Main problem: no sales, no conversion, no users, etc.
  • What you have tried so far.
  • What decision you need to make.
  • Plan you are interested in: Express, Full or Premium + Market.
This website does not store requests or customer data. Contact is handled by email. If the user does not finally send the email from their email application, no request will arrive.

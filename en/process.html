Work process

Product Autopsy AI follows a simple process to move from a confusing situation to a clear and actionable diagnosis.

The goal is for the client to know from the beginning what to send, what they will receive and how the final decision will be made.

Process phases

Initial request

The client sends the URL, product description, main problem and the plan they are interested in.

Fit review

We check whether the product fits Product Autopsy AI and recommend the most suitable plan: Express, Full or Premium + Market.

Questionnaire

If information is missing, the client completes the initial questionnaire: product, audience, problem, metrics, competition and goal.

Confirmation

Scope, price, delivery format and estimated timeline are confirmed before starting.

Analysis

We analyze value proposition, target customer, real pain, differentiation, trust, offer, conversion, viability and, if applicable, market.

Final report

The client receives a report with diagnosis, score, problems, opportunities, recommended actions and final verdict.

Clarification and feedback

The client may request a reasonable clarification and, if they wish, provide feedback about the usefulness of the report.

Estimated timing

Express Autopsy

48-72 hours after receiving sufficient information.

Full Autopsy

3-5 business days, depending on complexity and available material.

Premium + Market

5-10 business days, because it includes a strategic market study.

What the client should send

  • Product, landing page, app, demo or store URL.
  • Short product description.
  • Main problem.
  • Target customer.
  • Current price or offer.
  • Available metrics, if any.
  • Known competitors or alternatives.
  • Decision they need to make.

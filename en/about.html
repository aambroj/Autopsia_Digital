Who is behind it

About Product Autopsy AI

Product Autopsy AI / Autopsia Digital is a service created by Alberto Ambroj to help understand why a digital product is not selling, converting or getting users.

The approach

Most digital products do not fail only because of technology. Many fail because of a mix of unclear messaging, too broad an audience, weak offer, low trust, poor conversion or lack of differentiation.

Product Autopsy AI was created to review these areas in a structured way and turn confusion into a decision: relaunch, reposition, pause or shut down.

Service principles

Clarity over hype

No magic sales promises. The service delivers analysis, judgment and specific actions.

Practical decisions

The goal is not to write a long report, but to help decide what to do next.

Controlled cost

Designed for founders, makers, creators and small teams who need diagnosis without large consulting budgets.

Privacy

Real cases are handled privately. Client data is not published without explicit permission.

What kind of help it provides

  • Detect why a landing page does not convert.
  • Review whether an app has a clear value proposition.
  • Understand whether a product audience is too broad.
  • Compare an idea with visible market alternatives.
  • Prioritize changes before investing more time or money.
  • Decide whether to relaunch, reposition, pause or shut down.

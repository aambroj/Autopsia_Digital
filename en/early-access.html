What kind of products fit

Already launched products

Apps, websites, SaaS products or tools that already exist but are not getting sales, users or traction.

Landing pages with no conversion

Pages with visitors but no contacts, purchases, registrations or real requests.

Ideas before building

Digital ideas you want to validate before spending weeks or months building them.

Stalled products

Old or paused projects you are not sure whether to relaunch, reposition or shut down.

What you will receive

  • Clear diagnosis of the main problem.
  • Review of value proposition, audience, offer, pricing, trust and conversion.
  • Prioritized list of issues.
  • Recommended actions to improve.
  • Final verdict: relaunch, reposition, pause or shut down.

Launch condition

During this early phase, first cases will receive a closer review. The goal is to validate the methodology, improve the report format and build real experience across different digital products.

What the client receives

The result of a Product Autopsy is a practical, structured and decision-oriented report. It is not a decorative presentation: it is a tool to understand what is broken, what can still be saved and what to do next.

The goal of the deliverable is to turn confusion into a clear decision: relaunch, reposition, pause or shut down.

Report structure

1. Executive summary

Direct diagnosis of the product status, main problem and initial recommendation.

2. Product Autopsy Score

0 to 40 score across clarity, audience, real pain, differentiation, trust, offer, conversion and viability.

3. Problems detected

Prioritized list of blockers preventing the product from selling, converting or getting users.

4. Value proposition

Analysis of whether people understand what is being sold, who it is for and why they should pay.

5. Target customer

Review of ideal customer, segmentation, objections and possible more profitable niche.

6. Website, landing or app

Review of clarity, trust, friction, calls to action and conversion leaks.

7. Offer and pricing

Analysis of price, package, revenue model and perceived value.

8. Market and competition

The Premium plan includes strategic analysis of competitors, alternatives, pricing and opportunities.

9. Action plan

Specific actions for the next 7 days and the next 30 days.

10. Final verdict

Clear conclusion: relaunch, reposition, pause or shut down.

Delivery format

The report can be delivered in one or several of these formats:

  • Clean PDF ready to read.
  • Editable document if the client needs it.
  • Structured email for Express diagnoses.
  • Separate executive summary for quick decisions.
  • Prioritized action plan.

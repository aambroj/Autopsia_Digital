Strategic Market Study

The Premium plan of Product Autopsy AI can include a strategic market study to better understand your product’s commercial environment: competitors, alternatives, pricing, messaging, target customers and positioning opportunities.

This study is designed for digital products that need a serious decision: relaunch, reposition, change audience, modify the offer or shut the project down.

What is included

Competitor map

Identification of similar alternatives, adjacent products and indirect competitors.

Positioning analysis

Review of how other products present themselves and what space your product could occupy.

Pricing and models

Review of visible prices, revenue models, packages, freemium, subscription or one-time payment.

Target customer

Detection of possible segments and recommendation of a clearer niche to sell to.

Commercial messaging

Analysis of promises, headlines, benefits, objections and language used in the market.

Opportunities

Detection of gaps, differentiation angles and possible ways to reposition the product.

Final output

The client receives a specific section inside the Premium report with:

  • Market summary.
  • Main competitors or alternatives.
  • Positioning comparison.
  • Commercial risks detected.
  • Differentiation opportunities.
  • Target customer recommendation.
  • Pricing or offer recommendation, if applicable.
  • Relaunch or repositioning plan.

Study limits

The market study is based on strategic external research, public information, visible competitors, commercial messaging, published pricing and detectable opportunities. It does not include paid surveys, large-scale interviews, private database purchases or advanced statistical research.

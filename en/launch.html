What problem it solves

Many times the problem is not technical. The product may work, but fail commercially because:

  • The value proposition is not clear.
  • The target audience is too broad.
  • The price or offer does not fit.
  • The landing page does not create trust.
  • The product looks too similar to alternatives.
  • There is no clear reason to pay.

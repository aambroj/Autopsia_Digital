What is a Product Autopsy?

A Product Autopsy is an external, direct and practical review of a digital product that is not performing as expected. It is not a long technical audit. It is a strategic diagnosis focused on the reasons why people do not buy, sign up or understand the value.

The result is a clear report. You will know what is broken, what can still be saved, what should be changed first and whether it makes sense to keep investing time or money.

Designed for founders, makers, indie hackers, freelancers, small teams and creators who have launched something digital but are not getting sales, users or traction.

What we analyze

Value proposition

We check whether people can understand what you sell, who it is for and why it matters.

Landing page

We review clarity, trust, messaging, structure, calls to action and possible conversion leaks.

Offer and pricing

We analyze whether your price, package, promise or business model is blocking the sale.

Product

We detect whether the product solves a real problem, feels too generic or needs simplification.

Target customer

We review whether you are speaking to the right customer or trying to appeal to everyone.

Competition

We compare your product with existing alternatives to find weak points and positioning opportunities.

How it works

You send us your product

It can be a URL, screenshots, a demo, a short description or even an early-stage idea.

We analyze it like a real buyer

We look at clarity, trust, perceived value, pricing, friction, differentiation and objections.

You receive a clear report

The report includes diagnosis, key problems, opportunities, priority fixes and a final verdict.

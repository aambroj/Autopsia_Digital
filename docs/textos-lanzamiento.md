He lanzado una primera versión de Autopsia Digital.

La idea es sencilla:

- propuesta de valor confusa,
- público objetivo demasiado amplio,
- oferta mal planteada,
- falta de confianza,
- precio poco claro,

- qué falla,

## 2. LinkedIn - Inglés

I have launched the first version of Product Autopsy AI.

It is a strategic diagnosis service for digital products that are not selling, converting or getting users.

Many digital products do not fail because of technology.

They fail because of things that are harder to see from the inside:

- unclear value proposition,
- too broad target audience,
- weak offer,
- lack of trust,
- confusing pricing,
- poor differentiation,
- or no clear reason to pay.

Product Autopsy AI analyzes the product and provides a clear diagnosis:

- what is broken,
- what can still be saved,
- what should be changed,
- and whether to relaunch, reposition, pause or shut it down.

Website:
https://aambroj.github.io/Autopsia_Digital/en/

I am looking for early real cases to validate the methodology and improve the service.

## 3. Indie Hackers / Reddit - Inglés

Post:
Hi everyone,

I have launched a small productized service called Product Autopsy AI.

It is designed for founders, makers and small teams who have built a digital product but are not getting sales, users or traction.

The idea is to review:

- value proposition,
- landing page,
- target customer,
- offer and pricing,
- competition,
- trust signals,
- conversion leaks,
- and product viability.

The final output is a clear verdict:

- relaunch,
- reposition,
- pause,
